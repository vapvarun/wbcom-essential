# WooCommerce Widgets

WooCommerce Widgets

5 widgets for e-commerce features. Requires WooCommerce plugin.

These widgets only appear in Elementor when WooCommerce is installed and activated.

Universal Product

Display WooCommerce products with full styling control.

Key Settings:

Columns: 2-6 products wide
Per Page: Products to show
Category: Filter by category
Show: Image, title, price, rating, add to cart
Order By: Date, price, popularity, rating
Sale Badge: Show discounted items

Style Options:

Card styling (shadow, border, radius)
Image hover effects
Price typography
Button styling
Badge colors

Best For:

Featured products on homepage
Category landing pages
New arrivals sections
Sale product showcases

Customer Review

Display product reviews as testimonials.

Key Settings:

Product: Select product for reviews
Reviews Count: How many to show
Show Rating: Star display
Show Avatar: Reviewer photo
Show Date: When reviewed

Best For:

Product pages
Social proof sections
Trust-building areas

Product Tab

Display product information in tabs.

Key Settings:

Tabs: Description, Specs, Reviews
Custom Tabs: Add your own
Icon: Optional tab icons
Position: Top or Side tabs

Best For:

Product detail pages
Specification displays
Enhanced product info

Add Banner

Promotional banner for products.

Key Settings:

Image: Banner background
Title: Promotion text
Description: Details
Button: Shop now link
Link Product/Category: Direct link

Best For:

Homepage promotions
Sale announcements
Category features

WC Testimonial

Customer testimonials for products.

Key Settings:

Testimonials: Add multiple
Product Link: Associate with product
Show Rating: Star display
Layout: Cards or minimal

Best For:

Product-specific reviews
Customer success stories
Trust building
