## Production Notes

### Visual Guidelines

- Clean, uncluttered shots
- Realistic content (not lorem ipsum)
- Smooth transitions
- Text legible on mobile
- Include captions for sound-off viewing

### Pacing Guidelines

| Length | Pace |
|--------|------|
| 15s | Very fast, 3-4 scenes |
| 30s | Fast, 5-6 scenes |
| 60s | Moderate, 8-10 scenes |

### Call-to-Action Options

| CTA | Use When |
|-----|----------|
| "Download free" | Cold audience, awareness |
| "See all blocks" | Warm audience, consideration |
| "Try it now" | Hot audience, conversion |
| "Learn more" | Retargeting |

### A/B Testing Ideas

- Test problem-first vs. solution-first hooks
- Test showing vs. not showing pricing
- Test different CTAs
- Test BuddyPress focus vs. general WordPress focus
