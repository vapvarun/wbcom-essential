## Platform-Specific Versions

### YouTube Pre-Roll (15s)
- Use Ad 1 or Ad 2
- Skip button appears at 5s, so front-load the hook
- Include verbal CTA

### Instagram/Facebook Feed (15s)
- Use Ad 2 or Ad 3
- Design for silent viewing (text-heavy)
- Square format (1:1)

### Instagram/Facebook Stories (15s)
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Full-screen widget demos
- Swipe-up CTA

### LinkedIn (30s)
- Use Ad 5 (developer focus)
- Professional tone
- Focus on time/cost savings

### Twitter/X (30s)
- Use Ad 4 or Ad 6
- Quick cuts, high energy
- Clear branding

---

## Production Notes

### Visual Requirements
- All ads need brand colors (BuddyX blue)
- Consistent logo placement (lower right or end card)
- Clean WordPress/Elementor recordings
- Professional stock footage for lifestyle shots

### Audio Requirements
- Royalty-free music (upbeat, modern)
- Professional voiceover (clear, enthusiastic)
- Audio peaks under -3dB

### Text Requirements
- Large, readable text (mobile-friendly)
- High contrast for accessibility
- Brand fonts where possible

### File Formats to Deliver
- MP4 (H.264)
- Multiple aspect ratios: 16:9, 1:1, 9:16
- With and without voiceover
- With and without captions
