## Tutorial Posts

### How-To: Member Grid

**How to display your BuddyPress members in a professional grid layout**

Step by step (takes about 5 minutes):

1. **Add the block**
   Go to any page in the WordPress editor, click +, and search for "Members Grid"

2. **Choose your layout**
   Set columns (3-4 works best), enable "Theme Colors" to match your design

3. **Filter options**
   Show all members, specific member types, or filter by recent activity

4. **Publish**
   Preview on mobile (it adapts automatically) and hit publish

### How-To: Testimonials

**Adding testimonials to your WordPress site?**

Here's the easy way with the Testimonial Carousel block:

1. Add the Testimonial Carousel block to your page
2. Click "Add Testimonial"
3. Fill in: Name, Role/Company, Quote, Photo, Rating
4. Repeat for each testimonial
5. Adjust how many show at once

## Tips Posts

### Quick Tip: Theme Colors

**Quick tip for WBcom Essential users:**

Before you spend time customizing colors, try this:

1. Select any block
2. Find "Use Theme Colors" in the settings
3. Toggle it ON

The block will automatically use your theme's colors. If you're using Theme Reign, BuddyX, or BuddyX Pro, it integrates perfectly. Works with any theme that uses CSS variables too.

Saves a ton of time on color matching!

---

### Quick Tip: BuddyPress

**BuddyPress tip:**

If your members aren't completing their profiles, add the Profile Completion block to their dashboard.

It shows a progress bar and links to missing fields. Visual reminder = more complete profiles = better community.

Find it under Starter Pack - BuddyPress in the block inserter.

---

### Quick Tip: Sliders

**Not sure which slider block to use?**

Quick guide:

- **Post Carousel** - One post at a time, slideshow style
- **Posts Carousel** - Multiple posts visible, group scrolling
- **Post Slider** - Full-width, hero-style with image backgrounds
- **Slider** - Custom content slides (not posts)

Pick based on how much focus each item needs.
